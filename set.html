Set Crafting Guide

Discover the art of crafting in our game with this beginner's guide, focusing on equipment sets at levels 140, 170, 180, 185, and 190. Each tier presents unique challenges, requiring rare materials and intricate recipes. Delve into the cost analysis, comparing self-gathering to market purchases. The guide also features a visual showcase, highlighting the aesthetic appeal of each set. Equip yourself with the knowledge to navigate the crafting world, from the stylish level 140 set to the pinnacle of mastery at levels 185 and 190. Happy crafting!
